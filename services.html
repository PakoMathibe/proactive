OUR SERVICES

TAILOR MADE SOLUTIONS

We specialise in providing tailor made accounting solutions to small, medium and large enterprises. Our main aim is to offer our clients services of the highest standards through our specialized departments and to add value to their business activities. This is achieved through the in-depth knowledge of our clients' activities together with the experience and know-how of our people.

Accounting Services

Proactive Accountants can support your organisation in the areas of financial statements preparation, Maintenance of accounting records, Setting up of accounting systems, Annual financial statements, Management accounts, which will result in enhanced efficiency and quality reporting.

Business Advisory Services

Our Business Advisory team develops and offers tailored, innovative and comprehensive solutions for specific client business needs that may not require the expression of opinions based on professional standards. We aim to bring more focus to the value proposition by delivering a broader range of advisory services and competencies.

Taxation Services

Since corporate tax may create significant additional costs and cash outflows, we help our clients in assessing their tax challenges and opportunities, and develop right solutions to mitigate their tax burdens by understanding their specific business requirements. We work with our clients with the aim to reduce their tax burden to a minimum.
