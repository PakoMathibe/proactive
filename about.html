ABOUT PROACTIVE ACCOUNTANTS

Proactive Accountants is a business established in terms of Companies Act 71 of 2008 which consists of one sole director a qualified Professional Accountant (SA) and Professional Tax Practitioner a full member of South African Institute of Professional Accountant (SAIPA) & SAIBA within the accounting and financial sector. The company is 100% black woman owned and controlled entity that was established on the 07/06/2015.

Our Mission

Our mission is to add value in the mainstream economy through the involvement of all stakeholders and partners.

Our Objectives

Our primary objective of founding this entity is to address socio economic inequalities created by the historical past that created imbalances and the legacy that economic have to reverse.

To create a professional, successful and sustainable entity, with a significant asset base, by reliving or prospective client from non-core operations within the respective businesses.
